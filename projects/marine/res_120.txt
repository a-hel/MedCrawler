 Wilson Daily Times, 14 March 1919.  Henry Tart registered for the World War I draft on 18 September 1918. He recorded his address as the corner of Green and Reid Streets, his birth date as 11 April 1884, and his occupation as self-employed in the transfer business. His wife Julia C[lark] Tart was his next-of-kin, and he signed his card in a neat, well-spaced hand. Upon Henrysdeath, Tarts wife applied for Letters 0f Administration for her husbands estate. She listed foursurviving daughters, all minors  indeed, young children  Olivia, Julia, Josephine, and Miriam Tart.
